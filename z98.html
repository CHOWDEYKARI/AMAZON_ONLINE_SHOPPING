How do I create an Amazon account?
Creating an Amazon account is easy. Simply go to the Amazon website, click on 'Account & Lists' at the top right, and select 'Start here' under the 'New customer?' section. Follow the prompts to enter your details and create your account.
What should I do if I forgot my Amazon password?
If you've forgotten your password, go to the Amazon sign-in page and click on 'Forgot your password?'. Enter your email address or mobile number, and follow the instructions to reset your password.
How can I track my Amazon order?
To track your order, log in to your Amazon account and go to 'Your Orders'. Find the order you want to track and click on 'Track Package' to see the current status and estimated delivery date.
What is Amazon Prime and how can I sign up?
Amazon Prime is a membership program offering benefits such as free shipping, exclusive deals, and access to Prime Video. To sign up, go to the Amazon Prime page and click on 'Start your free 30-day trial' or 'Join Prime'. Follow the prompts to complete your subscription.
How do I return an item on Amazon?
